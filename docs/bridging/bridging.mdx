You can move tokens in and out of Etherlink through a process called _bridging_.
See these related pages for more information:

- [Bridging XTZ between Tezos layer 1 and Etherlink](/bridging/bridging-tezos)
- [Bridging tokens between Etherlink and other EVM networks](/bridging/bridging-evm)
- [Bridging FA tokens between Tezos layer 1 and Etherlink](/bridging/bridging-fa)

## Native bridges

Etherlink developers have created web-based bridges for different situations.
These bridges are permissionless; anyone can use them without restrictions or the intervention of a third party.
